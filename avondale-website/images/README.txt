Avondale Website — Image Placeholders
=====================================

Replace these files with your own (keep the same filenames):

  logo.png
    Band logo used in the top-left navigation.
    Tip: Use a transparent PNG (white or light logo works best
    over the dark hero overlay). Recommended width: ~200–320px.

  band-photo.jpg
    Full-width homepage hero image.
    Tip: Use a wide landscape photo of the band.
    Recommended size: at least 1920px wide.

  holding-patterns.jpg
    Album artwork on the Merch page.
    Tip: Square album cover works best.
    Recommended size: at least 1000 × 1000px.

How to replace an image:
  1. Export or save your new image with the exact filename above.
  2. Drag it into this "images" folder.
  3. When asked, choose Replace / Overwrite.
  4. Refresh the website in your browser.

The site will still load if an image is missing — you may just
see a blank area until you add the file.
